My Personal Career

I have wrestled since I was five years old. I started when my uncle invited me after he had just finished his high school carrer. I started out only winning two matches my first year of competing. After that year I took off winning multiple states titles and eventual national titles as well. As I entered junior high I took a year off from wrestling after burning out.

I wasn't sure if I would continue to wrestle in high school, but sure enough I continued. My placements in high school were as follows.

  • Freshman:5th

  • Sophmore:2nd

  • Junior:2nd

  • Senior:3rd
